About

I photograph the world in the spaces between planned and found.

Based in Norway, I've spent years documenting light — the way it moves across a fjord at 2am in summer, the way it cuts hard shadows through a city at noon, the softness it gives a stranger's face in an unguarded moment.

My work spans editorial, landscape, architecture, and portraiture. I believe in technical precision in service of emotional truth — no tricks, no heavy filters. Just light, composition, and patience.
